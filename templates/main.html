Unofficial Metlink Wellington Real Time Anywhere

A fast, bookmarkable app for getting RTI info for bus stops in Wellington that don't have displays. Enter the stop number below.

Stop numbers can be found on bus stop signs. For example Courntey Place Stop A (westbound) is stop number 5000, while stop C (opposite) is stop number 5516.
